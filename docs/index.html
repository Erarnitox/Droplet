Features:

Challenge Roles

Create CTF-Style Challenges and give out Roles as a Reward!

YouTube Notifications

Create custom YouTube-Upload notifications for your server!

CTF Leaderboard

Create CTF-Style Challenges and reward solves with exp and badges. Points will be synced across all Servers where Droplet is invited.

Flip a coin

`/flip` lets you flip a coin and gamble for exp.

Roll a dice

`/roll` lets you roll a dice and gamble for exp.

Help

`/help` will dynamically create a manual for you based on what commands are accessible to you.

Member Greetings

Newly joined Members will be greeted by the bot.

Goodbye!

Droplet says goodbye to members when they leave.

Reaction Roles

Let your members assign their own roles by reacting to a configurable message.

Server Details

Get a quick insight into your Server.

And even more!

Features are added and improved constantly, so why don't you invite it to your server and give it a try!
